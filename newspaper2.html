This is Stray Kids!!(Boy Group)

Stray Kids is a South Korean boy group formed by JYP Entertainment in 2017, consisting of eight members: Bang Chan (leader), Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N.

They debuted on March 25, 2018, with the EP I Am NOT and are recognized for their energetic performances and self-produced music that often explores themes of identity and youth. Notable songs include "God's Menu," "Back Door," "Thunderous," and "MANIAC."

Their official fanbase is called Stay, symbolizing the bond between the group and their fans. Stray Kids is known for their innovative sound and choreography, often incorporating elements of hip-hop, rock, and EDM into their music, making them stand out in the K-pop industry.

This is Ateez!!(Boy Group)

ATEEZ is a South Korean boy group formed by KQ Entertainment in 2018, consisting of eight members: Hongjoong (leader), Seonghwa, Yunho, Yeosang, San, Mingi, Wooyoung, and Jongho.

They debuted on October 24, 2018, with the EP Treasure and are celebrated for their powerful performances and self-produced music that often explores themes of identity and youth. Notable songs include "God's Menu," "Back Door," "Thunderous," and "MANIAC."

Their official fanbase is called ATINY, symbolizing that ATINYs are the destiny of ATEEZ. ATEEZ is known for their unique concept of "treasure hunting," where each album and song tells a cohesive story related to adventure and exploration.

This is Twomorrow X Together (TXT)!!(Boy Group)

TXT, short for Tomorrow X Together, is a South Korean boy group under HYBE (formerly BigHit Entertainment). They debuted on March 4, 2019, with the EP "The Dream Chapter: STAR," which included their hit song "CROWN." The group consists of Soobin (leader), Yeonjun, Beomgyu, Taehyun, and Huening Kai.

Known for their versatile music and captivating storytelling, TXT often explores themes of youth, self-discovery, and emotional struggles. They have evolved musically, blending pop, rock, and experimental sounds in songs like "Blue Hour," "0X1=LOVESONG (I Know I Love You)," and "Sugar Rush Ride."

Their official fanbase, MOA (Moments of Alwaysness), represents the unbreakable bond between the members and their fans. TXT continues to be a global trendsetter in K-pop, redefining what it means to be a 4th-generation powerhouse.

This is NewJeans!!(Girl Group)

NewJeans is a South Korean girl group under ADOR (a sub-label of HYBE) that debuted on July 22, 2022 with their surprise pre-release track "Attention." The group consists of Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein, who quickly captivated audiences with their fresh, nostalgic sound and Y2K-inspired visuals.

Unlike the typical flashy K-pop debut, NewJeans introduced a minimalistic and natural image, focusing on smooth R&B and pop melodies. Their hit songs "Hype Boy," "OMG," and "Ditto" became viral sensations, earning them millions of fans worldwide.

Their fanbase is called Bunnies, a playful representation of their youthful and free-spirited energy. With their unique aesthetic, unconventional promotions, and effortlessly cool sound, NewJeans is redefining the K-pop landscape.

This is BabyMonster!!(Girl Group)

BABYMONSTER is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, officially introduced in 2023. The group consists of seven members: Ahyeon, Haram, Rora, Minji, and more, showcasing a diverse range of talents and backgrounds.

This is Aespa!!(Girl Group)

Aespa is a South Korean girl group formed by SM Entertainment, debuting on November 17, 2020, with their single "Black Mamba." The group consists of four members: Karina (leader), Winter, Giselle, and Ningning.

Aespa is known for their unique concept that integrates virtual avatars, referred to as "ae," which represent each member in a digital world. Their music blends genres such as pop, R&B, and EDM, with notable tracks including "Next Level," "Savage," and "Illusion."

Their official fanbase is called MY, symbolizing the connection between the group and their fans. aespa's innovative approach to music and storytelling, alongside their stunning visuals and performances, has made them a prominent figure in the K-pop industry.
